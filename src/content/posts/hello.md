The internet moves quickly, so I wanted to keep one quieter place of my own.

This is where I will collect questions I am thinking through, small experiments, and glimpses of light, streets, and ordinary moments I would rather not forget. It does not need to be finished all at once. The site can find its shape slowly, alongside the life around it.

This is the first note, and the beginning of the archive.
